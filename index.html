    Kannada Poetry & Hobbies

    I love writing Kannada poetry and vibe coding in my free time. Here is a piece I wrote reflecting on learning and growth:

    ಕನ್ನಡ ಕವನ (Original Kannada)

    ಕಲಿಕೆಯ ಹಾದಿಯಲಿ ತಡವರಿಸಿ ನಡೆವಾಗ,
    ಬುದ್ಧಿಯ ಬೆಳಕು ಸದಾ ಜೊತೆಯಿರಲಿ.
    ಕನಸಿನ ಕಡಲಲಿ ಈಜುವ ಆಸೆಯಿರಲು,
    ಸಾಧನೆಯ ಶ್ರಮವೇ ದೋಣಿಯಾಗಲಿ.

    English Translation

    When faltering on the path of learning,
    May the light of intellect guide the way.
    When dreaming of swimming across the oceans,
    Let the struggle of effort be our vessel.
